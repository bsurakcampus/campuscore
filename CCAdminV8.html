📌 Important — Fees & Assessment Access
•Students are strongly advised to pay all scheduled fees within their due dates to avoid any delays, disturbance, and disruption in their academic journey.
•Students must secure DIGITAL CLEARANCE well in advance of each assessment deadline, to be allowed access to Submission Links for Summative Assessments at Ultra.
•Once clear by Accounts, Tutors will issue Submission Links within 24 Hrs.
•Please note that it may take 24–48 Hrs for Accounts to track and confirm your fee payment. Hence, please pay your fees well in advance of 1st & 2nd Assessment deadlines.
•LATE submissions are allowed within 4 days of the deadline, without any penalty.
•Tutors will close the Assessment Links after 4 days!
