📌 Important — Fees & Assessment Access
•Students are expected, and strongly advised to pay all scheduled fees within their due dates to avoid any delays, disturbance, and disruption in their academic journey.
•Students must secure DIGITAL CLEARANCE with Accounts well in advance of each Assessments, and Re-Assessments Deadlines, to access Submission Links at Ultra.
•Once clear by Accounts, Tutors will issue Submission Links within 24 Hrs.
•Please note that it may take 24–48 Hrs for Accounts to track and confirm your fee payment, and Tutors to issue the Submission Links. Hence, please pay your fees and secure Digital Clearance WELL IN ADVANCE of all Assessments, and Re-Assessments Deadlines.
•Please DO NOT send your invoices to Tutors — as they will follow Accounts data, and issue access as soon as possible. Follow up with Accounts at records@bathspa.ae for all fee payment related matters.
•LATE submissions are allowed within 2 days of the deadline, without any penalty.
•Tutors may accept late submissions after 2 days of original deadlines, but marks are capped to 40.
•Tutors will close Submission Links after 4 days!
🏦 For Accounts / Fee Related Matters
•To know your outstanding fees, please log in to your Skiply account.
•To request a fee statement, please email records@bathspa.ae
•For Skiply issues, please email records@bathspa.ae
•For ATM and bank transfer payments, please share your proof of payment to records@bathspa.ae
